What "local-first" means

Every calculation, every insight, every data transformation happens entirely on your device. Nothing is sent over the network. Munshiji has no servers to breach.

What we never collect

No account numbers. No balances. No transaction history. No identity. We cannot see your data because we never receive it — not even anonymised.

How your data enters Munshiji

Manual entry, file import (CSV/Excel), or bank statement PDF parsing — all processed on-device. You decide what goes in. You own what comes out.

Questions

Is Munshiji free?

Yes. Munshiji is free to use. There are no paywalls on core features. We may offer optional premium features in the future, but the core finance tracking will always be free.

How do you make money?

We don't monetise your data — ever. We plan to offer optional premium features (advanced reports, goal coaching) for a small one-time or subscription fee. Your data is yours, not ours to sell.

What happens to my data if my machine crashes?

Munshiji lets you export your data as an encrypted backup file at any time — save it to a USB drive, cloud storage, or anywhere you choose. Restore it on any machine. We never hold a copy.

Does it support Indian banks and instruments like SIP, PPF, NPS, ELSS?

Yes. Munshiji is built specifically for Indian financial instruments — SIPs, PPF, NPS, ELSS, FDs, RDs, gold bonds, real estate, and more. Indian number formatting (₹1,00,000) throughout.

Is Munshiji open source?

We are evaluating open-sourcing the core engine. Updates will be posted here. Our privacy commitment is unconditional regardless of open-source status.

How is this different from Walnut, MoneyView, or INDmoney?

Those apps require SMS/email access or account linking — your data goes to their servers. Munshiji is the opposite: everything stays on your device. We also go deeper on assets, insurance, and goal modelling than transaction-tracking apps.

Does it run on my laptop or desktop?

Yes — that's the primary use case. Munshiji runs entirely in your browser on any laptop or desktop. You can also self-host it on your own machine. Mobile browsers are supported too, so you can check your ledger on the go.

How do I import existing data?

You can import data via CSV/Excel files or bank statement PDFs. All parsing happens on your device. We also support manual entry for assets and instruments that can't be imported automatically.
