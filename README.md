# Munshiji

**Your Personal Finance Ledger-Keeper**

A self-hosted personal finance dashboard built for India. Track net worth, investments, loans, insurance, salary, and financial goals — with first-class support for EPF, NPS, PPF, SSY, and 13+ Indian account types.

---

## Why Munshiji?

Managing personal finances in India means juggling EPF statements, NPS tiers, PPF passbooks, mutual fund folios, insurance policies, and payslips with 15+ line items. Generic finance apps don't understand these instruments. Spreadsheets break at scale.

Munshiji is a single dashboard that speaks Indian finance natively — from HRA breakdowns in your payslip to wealth health scoring that factors in your emergency fund, insurance adequacy, and debt-to-asset ratio. Fully self-hosted, all calculations run locally — your financial data never leaves your machine.

## Features

### Dashboard & Net Worth
Track your complete financial picture at a glance — KPIs, net worth trends, asset distribution across equity, debt, commodity, hybrid, and cash. View portfolio returns, income distribution, and a full balance sheet. Option to exclude retirement accounts for liquid net worth view.

### Accounts
Manage **13+ account types**: Bank, Brokerage, NPS Tier 1 & 2, EPF, EPS, Credit Card, Loan (Home/Vehicle/Personal/Education), FD, RD, PPF, SSY, and Cash. Track interest rates, maturity dates, EMI amounts, and active/inactive status. See net position, total assets, total liabilities, and monthly EMI at a glance.

### Investment Portfolio
Track stocks and mutual funds across brokerage accounts. Monitor unrealised P&L, performance charts with period filters (3M, 6M, 12M, YTD), and asset class allocation. Import holdings from broker statements (CAS/CSV/Excel/PDF). Monthly snapshot tracking for historical performance.

### Income & Payslips
Record employments (FTE, contractor, consultant, intern) with UAN and PF account numbers. Detailed payslip management with **14+ earnings fields** (base salary, HRA, LTA, special allowance, flexible pay) and **deductions** (EPF, VPF, NPS, professional tax, TDS, ESI, LWF). YTD summaries per employer with payslip prefilling.

### Insurance
Track Health, Term Life, Critical Illness, Disability, Vehicle, and Home policies. Monitor sum assured, premiums, payment frequency, nominees, and policy periods. Flag employer-provided policies. See total active policies, coverage, and annual premium.

### Wealth Health
Composite **financial health score (0-100)** based on debt-to-asset ratio, solvency, and month-over-month growth. Three analysis views:
- **Wealth Health** — 12-month net worth trend, monthly change, asset allocation, debt trend, burn rate
- **Safety Net** — Emergency fund adequacy, life insurance vs 10x income benchmark, health coverage, employment security
- **Cash Flow** — Monthly inflow, investment, debt paydown, and surplus tracking

### Financial Goals
Set financial goals and map them to assets **across profiles** — each asset gets a contribution weightage (must sum to 100%). Track progress against targets with velocity-based ETA estimation using last 3 months of history. Goals span your entire Nest, not just one profile.

### Insights
Locally computed financial insights based on standard formulas — debt-to-solvency ratio, liquidity analysis, and other metrics derived from your assets, liabilities, expenses, and budget. **All calculations run on your device. No data leaves your machine.**

### Loan Analyser
Amortization calculator with auto-generated loan snapshots — each month's balance automatically computed as previous balance minus EMI.

### Nest (Multi-Profile)
A Nest groups multiple financial profiles — individual family members, households, or separate financial personas. Each profile has its own accounts, holdings, and snapshots. The **Nest Overview** provides a combined dashboard across all profiles, giving a unified view of total household net worth and finances. Switch between profiles from the sidebar.
